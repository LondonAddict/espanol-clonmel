Clonmel Spanish Classes
Group 2025–2026 · Level A1

This group started from zero in September 2025 and has been meeting every Tuesday for conversational, grammar-based classes. Over the course of the year we have completed the full A1 (Inicial) level — covering greetings, everyday vocabulary, key grammar structures, and the past tense — and attempted our first DELE A1 practice exam in May 2026. A1 typically takes 60–80 hours of classroom time. Our group has covered that content across 23 sessions, supplemented by homework and online resources.
